Before setting foot on the professor's house, he asked for help from a cyber community. He wanted their assistance in getting examination questions. The community came up with a plan to help him achieve his goal, but first, they told him to follow their secret Instagram page, which he discovered through a QR code. By scanning the code and following the page, he would learn how to start his mission . With the cyber community as his guide, he began his journey filled with cryptic clues and enigmatic messages, all leading him closer to his goal.
