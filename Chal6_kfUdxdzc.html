Karim closely examined the books on the shelf. Attached to each book was a number, indicating its significance in unlocking the office. It became apparent that there was a hidden word concealed within these numbers, serving as the password.

Karim noticed that the professor used only numbers with "*" and "#" symbols . This observation was the key to knowing the password.
